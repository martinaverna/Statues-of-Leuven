Rembert Dodoens

Location: Kapucijnenvoer 30, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Ad Wouters
Year: 2017

Rembert Dodoens , better known by his Latinized name Rembertus Dodonaeus (Mechelen , June 29, 1517 or 1518 - Leiden , March 10, 1585 ), was a renowned Flemish botanist and doctor.
He was the author of the 'Cruydeboeck, a vernacular translation of the German-language botanical dictionary by Leonhart Fuchs, but supplemented with his own findings. Dodoens was also an alumnus of Leuven University. In 1535 he obtained his master's degree in medicine at the age of eighteen. The statue was made by artist Ad Wouters and it is to be found in Leuven's Bothanical Gardens (Krudtuin).
