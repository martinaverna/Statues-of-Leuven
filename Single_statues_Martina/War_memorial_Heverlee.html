Oorlogsmonument Heverlee

Location: Waversebaan, 3001 Heverlee
Material: Bronze
Creator: Ernest Bastin
Year: 1919

Set in a bush-planted park, a memorial war statue in the shape of a statue depicting a bronze-cast soldier advancing with his head raised and a flag on the left, placed on a tall, hard stone base with a slightly conical, square shaft and ditto pillow. The base is decorated with oak branches, the shaft with hanging festoons at the corners, and the cushion with bundled laurel branches.
The pedestal has various inscriptions: on the front "HEVERLEE / ON HIS / GLORIERYKE / CHILDREN / DIED / FOR THE / FATHERLAND / 1914-1918 /" and "NO YOU WILL NEVER DIVIDE / WHAT A VAND SHOWS AND POWS ./ FREEDOM; FREEDOM; HERITAGE OF THE BELGIANS / BLOOD PURCHASED WITH THE FATHER./ DE LAET "; on the back side.
