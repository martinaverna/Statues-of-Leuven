Kamerood sesteg

Location: Sint-Donatuspark, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Jan Rosseels
Year: 2000

This work of art harks back to the Leuven expression 'camrode sixty', whose meaning nobody can now remember.
The Leuven Men of 60 commissioned the artist Jan Rosseels to dedicate a statue to it. They donated it then to the town in 2000, on the occasion of their 40th birthday. This statue of a young man with a burning desire to live is extremely symbolic.
