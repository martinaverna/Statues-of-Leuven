Statues of the Stadhuis

Location: Grote Markt, 3000 Leuven
Material: Stone
Creator: ---
Year: From 1850

The 15th century gothic Town Hall was in the 19th and early 20th century adorned with 236 statues which represent the Leuven "Hall of fame":

- Ground floor: artists, scholars and eminent citizens of Leuven.
- First floor: figures who symbolise the municipal privileges and the patron saints of the parishes.
- Second floor: the Counts of Leuven and the Dukes of Brabant.
- Turrets: biblical figures.
