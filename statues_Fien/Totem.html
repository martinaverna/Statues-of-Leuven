Totem (de kever)

Location: Ladeuzeplein, 3000 Leuven
Material: Polyester
Creator: Jan Fabre
Year: 1991

The giant totem opposite the university library was commissioned in 2004 by the K.U. Leuven manufactured by Jan Fabre. On a 23-meter-high needle, a huge jewel beetle (the memory of nature) adorns the university library (human memory). The artwork symbolizes the interconnectedness and perfect cooperation between the university and the city of Leuven on the occasion of its 575th anniversary. In addition, the totem pays tribute to the knowledge, beauty, science and poetry of existence.
