Kotmadam

Location: Oude markt, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Fred Bellefroid
Year: 1985

The statue was inaugurated on May 16, 1985. It was a gift from the VVV to the city of Leuven (worth Bfrs 800,000), but an original idea from the Marktrock committee. The inauguration twenty years ago was attended by Maria Swerts, the then oldest Kotmam in Leuven and also godmother of the statue. Despite the amusing character of the statue, the Kotmadam initially evoked mixed feelings. For example, the figure depicted did not correspond to the typical image of a kotma dam: old and curvy. On the contrary, the kotma dam on the Oude Markt is a youthful, attractive lady. Anyway, over the years the statue became an eye-catcher and many people have already sat down next to her or on her lap.
