Abraham

Location: Sint-Donatuspark, 3000 Leuven
Material: Stone
Creator: Freddy Janssens
Year: 1975

Abraham is the symbol of wisdom and of the men who have reached the age of 50. In 1975, at the request of the Mannen van het Jaar (Men of the year), the statue of Abraham, made by the artist Freddy Janssens, was installed in the Broekstraat and later, for reasons visibility and after a 'happening', moved to the entrance of the municipal park.
