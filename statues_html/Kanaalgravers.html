Kanaalgravers

Location: Vaartkom, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Willy Peeters
Year: 2000

On the 250th anniversary of the cutting of the first sod on the Leuven-Dyle canal (2000), the working party placed a permanent memory of the excavation works carried out by 500 workers on the Vaartkom. The bronze statue was cast by the Belgian artist Willy Peeters.
It is habit that sometimes the six canal diggers are dressed with knitted clothes.
