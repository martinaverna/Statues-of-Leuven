Leeuwenpaar

Location: Hogeschoolplein, 3000 Leuven
Material: Stone
Creator: Olivier Strebelle
Year: 1992

The inner courtyard of the Pope's College on Hogeschoolplein is ‘guarded’ by two fearsome looking lions, sitting to the left and right of the monumental stairway.
These animals were part of an exhibition by Belgian artist Olivier Strebelle organized in 1992 at the Pope's College. President of the college in office at the time, professor Sabbe, thought that the lions had to this point their place in this curtyard that he set out the fundraising necessary to purchase these sculptures. After four years, he had enough sponsors to pay the price - still kept secret today.
