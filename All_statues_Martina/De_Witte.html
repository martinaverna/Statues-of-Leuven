De Witte

Location: Blijde Inkomststraat 21, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: René Rosseel
Year: 1986

De Witte is the main character in the Belgian writer Ernest Claes' novel with the same name. He is a humorous scamp who plays a host of tricks and drives his parents to despair. The statue of 'De Witte' in Leuven, seen through the eyes of sculptor René Rosseel is to be found in in the garden of the the Faculty of Letters.
