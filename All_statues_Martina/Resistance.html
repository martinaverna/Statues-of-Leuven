Nationaal Monument van de Weerstand

Location: Dirk Boutslaan, 3000 Leuven
Material: Stone
Creator: Willem De Backer
Year: 1959

The statue depicts a woman with a dying son in her arms, overlooked by a man, representing a defender standing upright behind the suffering population.
It commemorates a part of the Leuven Resistance during World War II and it was placed on the right bank of the river Dijle on the initiative of the Nationale Koningsgezinde Beweging Leuven on 4th October 1959.
