De Lopers

Location: Tervuursevest 101, 3001 Heverlee
Material: Bronze
Creator: Mariette Teugels
Year: 1980

De Lopers (meaning The Runners in English) is a group of three statues representing two young girls and a boy engaged in a strenuous running. The sculptures are to be found in the sports fields of the De Nayer sports hall, at the University Sports Center of KU Leuven.
