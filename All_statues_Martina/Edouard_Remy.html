Edouard Remy

Location: Place Herbert Hoover, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Pieter Braecke
Year: 1899

Remy was the typical example of a 19th century paternalistic and socially engaged business man. He gave work to hundreds of people in his rice and starch factory in Wijgmaal, a sub-borough of Leuven. The decision to honor this great man dedicating a statue to him was taken at the end of 19th century. The innovative project was designed by the architect art nouveau Victor Horta and executed by Pieter Braecke.
