Vallend Paard

Location: Blijde Inkomststraat 21, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Rik Poot
Year: 1989

The statue of the Vallend Paard (Falling Horse in English) was made by Belgian artist Rik Poot in 1989 for the exhibition 'Sculpture after sculpture' at the K.U.Leuven. It is located in the courtyard of the Erasmushuis, the faculty of "Letteren en Wijsbegeerte".
