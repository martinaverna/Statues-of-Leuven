Van de Weyer

Location: Kapucijnenvoer, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Charles Geef
Year: 1876

Sylvain Van De Weyer of Leuven was the very first Belgian Prime Minister of Foreign Affairs. He was also member of the provisional government during the Belgian Revolution, reason enough for the city of Leuven to dedicate a statue to him in 1876.
From 1876 on, the statue by Charles Geefs stood in the middle of Ladeuzeplein. However, it had to be moved to the Kapucijnenvoer because it was too heavy for the new car park beneath.
