Pieter de Somer

Location: Charles Deberiotstraa, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Vic Gentils
Year: 1989

This statue was inaugurated on September 26, 1989. Pieter De Somer was the very first rector of the Flemish Catholic University of Leuven (1970). Pieter De Somer, usually called Piet De Somer, (Niel, December 22, 1917 - Leuven, June 17, 1985) was a Belgian professor, physician, biologist and first rector of the independent Dutch-speaking Catholic University of Leuven.
