Vriendschapsballon


Location: Martelarenplein, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Dany Tulkens
Year: 1988

In 1988 this statue was donated to the city by the Verbond der Jaartallen, a Leuven association. It is a work by Danny Tulkens. When the statue was first unveiled in 1988, no balloon was hanging over it due to stability issues. This was solved some time later with the help of KU Leuven, after which a second disclosure took place, but WITH balloon this time. In 2006, one of the persons was stolen in the basket. A reason for Danny Tulkens to renew all the statues and replace them with much heavier bronze statues instead of copper. The redesigned version was unveiled in 2007.
