ABOUT



Our website, entitled Statues of Leuven, is a dynamic web application that allows users to find the statues in the city of Leuven. The target audience covers travellers, culture lovers, international students as locals and students from Belgium. The main goal is to let the user discover the history of Leuven through the history, stories and legends of its statues.

We are both interested in culture and history since we are graduated in History (Martina) and Art History (Fien) and we both are curious travellers interested in the treasures which are hidden in small details. We found out that there is still no general website with all the statues of Leuven and their location with some background information - so we decided to make one.















Designed and built by Martina & Fien using just CSS and Bootstrap
