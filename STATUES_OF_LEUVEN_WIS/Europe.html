De ontvoering van Europa


Location: Provincieplein, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: Rik Poot
Year: 2008

The statue represents the god Zeus disguised as a bull kidnapping Europe, the daughter of the Phoenician royal couple Agenor and Telephessa. A famous scene from Greek mythology.
