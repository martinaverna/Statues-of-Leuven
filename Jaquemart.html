Jacquemart

Location: Grote markt, 3000 Leuven
Material: Bronze
Creator: /
Year: 1988

In the gable there is a clock, on the flat covered staircase next to it is a jacquemart (mechanical clock with figure of whistleblower) placed in 1998. A Jaquemart or Jacquemart is a clock in which the hours are beaten by one or more mechanical dolls. Usually the doll hits the clock with a hammer. The dolls themselves are also called Ja (c) quemart. The name probably comes from Dijon.
